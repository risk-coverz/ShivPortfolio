ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Shiv Patel.

I am a third-year Aerospace Engineering student at Toronto Metropolitan University, driven by a passion for designing and building systems that push the boundaries of flight and exploration. My academic and project work focuses on aerodynamics, propulsion systems, and advanced CAD modeling.

Beyond the classroom, I am an active member of the engineering community. From designing high-powered rockets with the Metropolitan Aerospace Rocket Society (MARS) to developing award-winning glider concepts and mentoring future engineers, I thrive in collaborative, problem-solving environments.

My goal is to leverage my technical skills and innovative mindset to contribute to the future of the aerospace industry, working on cutting-edge projects that make a real-world impact.
